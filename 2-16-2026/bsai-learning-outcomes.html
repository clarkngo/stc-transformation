BS in Artificial Intelligence (BSAI)

A Technical, Engineering-Focused Degree for the 2026 Labor Market

Program Pillars

1. Engineering Foundation
Focus on the "silicon-to-cloud" pipeline, including OS, Networking, and Cloud Computing.
2. Cognitive Architecture
Semantic intelligence through NLP, Generative AI, and Deep Learning.
3. Autonomy & Action
The "Agentic Edge": MLOps, Physical AI, and Digital Twins.

Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs)

  • PLO 1: Systems Integration: Architect scalable solutions across OS and Cloud.
  • PLO 2: Model Development: Design and optimize neural networks using vector calculus.
  • PLO 3: Generative AI: Build context-aware systems using LLMs and Vector Databases.
  • PLO 4: Autonomous Systems: Orchestrate multi-agent systems for real-time adaptation.
  • PLO 5: Operational Excellence: Manage the end-to-end machine learning lifecycle (MLOps).
  • PLO 6: Physical AI: Bridge digital intelligence with robotics via Digital Twins.
  • PLO 7: Ethics & Security: Evaluate the "Cyber-AI" threat landscape and algorithmic bias.
  • PLO 8: Technical Communication: Document complex agentic workflows and "code-as-spec."
