CityUX Open Ecosystem

TOFU → MOFU → BOFU → Conversion funnel. Plan to demo several user stories and journeys live.

Demo ready
What it is

A continuous feedback loop, not a one-way marketing funnel: public content and community engagement surface real market signals (what people are actually asking for), which get refined through hands-on formats, and feed directly into CityU's core academic structure. Everything in the ecosystem — YouTube, GitHub, Open edX, workshops, seminars, the community hub — points back to one core hub: CityU's courses, programs, syllabi, and faculty, tied together by a unified SSO/LTI identity so one learner profile follows a person across every node.

The funnel — four stages

TOFU · Discovery (YouTube) → MOFU · Hands-on (GitHub + Open edX) → BOFU · High-touch (Workshops, Seminars, Community Hub) → Conversion · Formal CityU enrollment

Six touchpoint nodes, plus one supply-side piece

🎥 YouTube 🐙 GitHub 🎓 Open edX 🛠️ Workshop 🎤 Seminar 💬 Community Hub 📘 Ebook / Gated Content live 🎥 Instructor Enablement live

Each node has its own strategy, technical integration, and available KPIs in the full plan. Instructor Enablement is the odd one out — it's the supply side: training CityU faculty who are already industry professionals to publish content, which is what fuels the other six nodes in the first place.

Six user journeys — not one straight line

  • 🎥 The Self-Directed Learner — the default path: organic YouTube discovery → GitHub/Open edX → Community Hub/Workshop → program registration.
  • 🎙️ Discovered via Industry Practitioner Content — finds CityUX through a practitioner's own content, not an official channel; branches depending on whether that practitioner is on Open edX yet.
  • 🚔 The Frontline Professional — e.g. an SPD officer, reached through an institutional partnership; skips discovery entirely, converts into a department-recognized credential, not a degree.
  • 💬 The Community-First Explorer — enters through word of mouth or a Discord invite, "out of order" — proof every touchpoint has to work as its own entry point.
  • 📘 The Ebook-First Lead — discovers CityUX via a gated ebook (LinkedIn, seminar handout); the one journey where identity is captured at the first touch, not the last.
  • 🌏 The Prospective International Student — reached via an overseas partnership, culminating in an in-person Seattle visit; highest-value outcome (full enrollment), longest cycle. Real example: the iKW Korea Summer Program.
