Course Design & Pedagogical Strategy

Open edX Open edX Tutor XBlocks
Format
Self-paced digital credential courses, built as 5 modules each.
Infrastructure
Open edX as the core platform, referenced alongside Open edX Tutor and XBlocks for the quiz/interactive layer.
Pedagogical model: competency-based learning built on "learning by doing" and "learning by concept," applying a flipped Bloom's Taxonomy approach rather than the traditional bottom-up sequence.
Instructional video

Short instructional / concept videos, roughly 7 minutes each.

Assessment

Embedded video quizzes built directly into the viewing experience.

Hands-on

Paired hands-on exercises reinforcing each concept.

🚔

Law Enforcement / Public Sector AI Program

Partnership
A collaborative training initiative built jointly with a police department partner (e.g. SPD) as the launch collaborator.
Model
Asynchronous skill-up model focused on practical, AI-enabled tools and compliance — deliberately not complex AI engineering.
Participant structure
Industry leaders and chiefs serve as instructors in the asynchronous model, supported behind the scenes by instructional content developers.
📣

Marketplace & Outreach Strategy

Distribution
Publish and promote lightweight course content across external platforms — Udemy, YouTube, LinkedIn — to drive traffic back to the core institution offerings.
Community
Student discussion channels and support groups on platforms like LinkedIn or Discord, specifically to keep self-paced courses from becoming simple "click-through" exercises.
