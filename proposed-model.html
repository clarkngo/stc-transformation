Proposed Curriculum Model: AI-Empowered Portfolio

Philosophy: Assess higher-order skills (Application, Synthesis, Architecture). AI is a tool students are expected to use transparently.

Status: Optimized for efficiency, reduced QA scope, and AI-resistant assessment.

Graded Component Breakdown (100%)

Applied Case Studies / Conceptual Assessment (40%)

Focus: Conceptual Mastery. **Mitigation:** Utilizes personalized, niche, and multi-modal prompts. Integrates mandatory **Oral Defense (Viva)** sessions for integrity.

Team Project: Architecture & Portfolio (40%)

Focus: System Design. Requires a formal portfolio of design documents, architecture choices, and a final product defense. Assesses complexity and design, not just functioning code.

Programming Exercises (PE) (20%)

Focus: AI-Aided Development. Requires students to submit a reflection log detailing **Prompt Engineering** and debugging of AI-generated code. Assesses management, not code production.

Non-Graded Components (Scaffolding & Support)

HOS (Hands-on Skills) (0%)

Role: Mandatory prerequisite practice. Efficiency: Student Workers are reassigned from grading this to curriculum review and improvement.

Concept/Project Discussion Forums (0%)

Role: Repurposed for mandatory Project Architecture discussions, peer feedback, and instructor-led Q&A.
