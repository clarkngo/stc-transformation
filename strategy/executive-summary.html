Executive Briefing: BSAI Program Launch

Goal: Launch a market-leading BS in Artificial Intelligence that prioritizes "Autonomy and Action" over traditional "Insights and Inference."

1. The Strategic Pivot

We are shifting from a theory-heavy curriculum to an Engineering-First model. This includes replacing standalone Generative AI core requirements with a rigorous Machine Learning & Data Mining foundation to ensure students can build the underlying "logic engines" for autonomous agents.

2. Resolving the "Math Gap"

To meet accreditation standards and industry requirements, we are integrating Computational Mathematics directly into the Python-first track. CS 251 (Statistical Computing) will now serve as a bridge for Linear Algebra and Vector Calculus using Tensors, ensuring students are prepared for the mathematical rigor of Deep Learning.

3. Market Differentiator: Physical AI & OT

Unlike standard CS degrees, our program focuses on Operational Technology (OT). Coursework in Physical AI and Digital Twins prepares graduates for high-growth roles in securing and managing industrial robotics and smart infrastructure—a "blue ocean" in current undergraduate offerings.
